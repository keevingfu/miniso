Content Distribution Strategy

Key Insights

  • Content Creator Distribution:
    • KOC dominance (60%) indicates strong emphasis on authentic user-generated content
    • KOL contribution (40%) provides professional content quality and reach
    • Platform distribution shows TikTok leading KOC engagement (25%), followed by Instagram (20%)
  • Audience Engagement Patterns:
    • Students show highest cross-platform engagement (29% total), particularly active on TikTok and YouTube
    • Fashion Enthusiasts demonstrate strong Instagram presence (15%) with diverse product interests
    • Young Parents and Collectors represent emerging segments with specific product preferences
    • Budget Shoppers show significant engagement on TikTok and Instagram (15% combined)
  • Product Category Dynamics:
    • IP Products (Blind Boxes & Plush Toys) drive collector engagement and gift purchases
    • Beauty Tools and Storage Items show strongest cross-segment appeal
    • Digital Gadgets and Stationery form core student and office worker essentials
    • Travel Accessories emerging as lifestyle integration category
  • Scenario Effectiveness:
    • Study Space and Work Setup combine for highest practical usage (33%)
    • Collection Display and Gift Giving dominate IP product scenarios
    • Beauty Routine shows highest single-category concentration
    • Daily Organization appears as key cross-category scenario

Strategic Recommendations

1. Platform-Specific Content Strategy

  • TikTok (35% Total Flow):
    • Prioritize KOC-led content (25%) focusing on quick product demonstrations
    • Target student audience with study-life integration content
    • Develop budget-friendly product showcases and hacks
  • Instagram (35% Total Flow):
    • Balance KOC/KOL content for authentic yet polished presentation
    • Focus on fashion enthusiasts and lifestyle content
    • Showcase aesthetic product arrangements and styling tips
  • YouTube (30% Total Flow):
    • Equal KOC/KOL distribution for comprehensive content
    • Create detailed product reviews and organization guides
    • Target collectors with unboxing and display content

2. Audience-Product Matching Strategy

  • Primary Segments:
    • Students: Create IP Blind Box + Stationery bundles with storage solutions
    • Fashion Enthusiasts: Develop beauty tool sets with travel-friendly features
    • Office Workers: Design integrated workspace organization systems
  • Growth Segments:
    • Young Parents: Bundle IP plush toys with practical storage solutions
    • Collectors: Create display-ready packaging for blind boxes and plush toys
    • Budget Shoppers: Develop value packs across beauty and stationery categories

3. Scenario-Based Marketing Approach

  • Functional Spaces:
    • Study Space: Integrate IP elements into functional study tools
    • Work Setup: Focus on aesthetic yet practical organization
    • Daily Organization: Cross-promote storage solutions with all categories
  • Lifestyle Integration:
    • Beauty Routine: Create complete beauty tool sets with travel options
    • Room Decoration: Combine IP products with home decor elements
    • Travel Essentials: Develop multi-functional travel accessories
  • Special Occasions:
    • Gift Giving: Design special packaging for IP products
    • Collection Display: Create modular display solutions for collectors
    • Seasonal Promotions: Align product bundles with occasions
