Short Video Strategy and Scripting for Social Media

Short Video Creation Directions

This section outlines content creation directions for different MINISO product categories, targeting specific audiences and themes to maximize engagement on short video platforms.

Product Category Target Audience Creation Themes
Figurines/Dolls Young Women, Families, Collectors
  • Theme 1: "Day with a Healing Plush Toy" - Daily life scenes with the doll.
  • Theme 2: "Blind Box Opening Challenge" - Surprise unboxing to attract viewers.
  • Theme 3: "IP Collaboration Series" - Character stories with popular IPs.
Plush Toys Families, Parents and Children
  • Theme 1: "Parent-Child Interaction Time" - Parents and kids playing with MINISO toys.
  • Theme 2: "Doll DIY Decoration" - Home decoration ideas using MINISO dolls.
  • Theme 3: "New Product Preview" - Showcase new plush toys and details.
Blind Boxes Collectors, Young People
  • Theme 1: "Blind Box Challenge" - Multi-person unboxing competition.
  • Theme 2: "Blind Box Series Story" - Serial short videos with blind box characters.
  • Theme 3: "Limited Edition Reveal" - Focus on rare and special edition blind boxes.
Bags Fashionable Women, Students
  • Theme 1: "Bag Versatile Outfit Guide" - Styling guide with MINISO bags.
  • Theme 2: "One Bag, Multiple Uses" - Practicality in different scenarios.
  • Theme 3: "New Product Launch" - Showcase design details in a dynamic video.

Example Short Video Scripts

Example Script - Figurines/Dolls Category: "Day with a Healing Plush Toy"

Title: "Day with a Healing Plush Toy"
Scene 1 (Morning):
    Scene: Sunlight in bedroom, doll on nightstand.
    Text/Voiceover: "Waking up in the morning, having it with me makes every day a good mood."
Scene 2 (Work):
    Scene: Doll on desk, owner smiles touching it while working.
    Text/Voiceover: "Busy work also needs a little healing."
Scene 3 (Evening):
    Scene: Owner holding doll on sofa watching TV.
    Text/Voiceover: "The day ends, it's complete with its company."
Ending Scene (Close-up):
    Scene: Doll close-up, background text: "MINISO plush toys, heal your every day."
Music: Warm light music.

Example Script - Blind Box Category: "Blind Box Opening Challenge"

Title: "Blind Box Opening Challenge"
Scene 1 (Opening):
    Scene: Two participants holding MINISO blind boxes, ready to open.
    Text/Voiceover: "Today we are having a blind box opening challenge!"
Scene 2 (Unboxing):
    Scene: Participants unpack step-by-step, focus on unboxing actions and facial expressions.
    Text/Voiceover: "Who will draw the hidden edition?"
Scene 3 (Result Reveal):
    Scene: One participant draws hidden edition, cheers; another draws regular, feigns disappointment.
    Text/Voiceover: "The hidden edition is so exciting!"
Ending Scene (Product Display):
    Scene: Display full series of blind box characters, with purchase link or event info.
Music: Lively and cheerful music.

Data Collection & Analysis for Short Videos

Effective short video content relies on continuous data analysis and strategy optimization. Key aspects include:

Core Data Metrics

Metric Description Purpose
Watch Time & Completion Rate Duration viewers watch & percentage who watch to the end. Indicates content attractiveness.
Likes, Comments, Shares User interaction metrics. Reflects user engagement and content resonance.
User Retention Rate Rate of users returning to view more content. Shows long-term content appeal.
User Behavior Analysis Analysis of viewing habits and preferences. Understand audience behavior for content tailoring.

Content Strategy Optimization

Strategy Description Benefit
Account Positioning Adjustment Maintain content verticality and platform recognition. Accurate platform categorization & audience targeting.
Precise Audience Targeting Target audience based on user profiles. Content tailored to specific user interests.
Content Planning Establish mid-to-long term content plans. Algorithm learning time and consistent content delivery.
Interaction Enhancement Optimize video openings, control video length, add interactive elements. Improved completion rates and user participation.
Interactive Guidance Strengthen comment interactions, understand user needs, organize engagement activities. Enhanced user engagement and community building.

Basic Composition Elements for Short Videos

Creating effective short videos involves careful consideration of several basic composition elements to ensure engagement and clarity.

Element Description Purpose
Duration Specific length for each shot. Facilitates editing and pacing.
Shot Size (景别) Varying angles (Long shot, Full shot, Medium shot, Close-up, Extreme close-up). Provides visual variety and focus.
Screen Content (画面内容) Specific scenes and content to be presented. Clear visual storytelling.
Shooting Method (拍摄方式) Techniques like push, pull, pan, move, follow. Dynamic visual presentation.
Subtitles & Dialogue (字幕与台词) Text content and dialogues in the video. Clarity and accessibility for viewers.
Props Preparation (道具准备) Necessary props for shooting. Enhances visual elements and themes.
Music & Sound Effects (音乐与音效) Video background music and sound design. Emotional tone and engagement.

Structure Design & Creation Skills

Aspect Description Benefit
Three-Stage Layout (三段式布局) Introduction (引子), Development (承接), Climax (高潮). Engaging structure with quick hook, content expansion, and impactful conclusion.
Opening Hook (开场钩子) Capture attention in the first 3-7 seconds. Maximizes initial viewer engagement.
Plot Design (情节设计) Incorporate suspense and reversals. Improved video completion rate.
Personal Labels (个人标签) Develop unique content style and personal brand. Distinct content identity and viewer recognition.
Concise Language (语言简洁) Use simple vocabulary and sentence structures. Easy understanding and broader audience reach.
Clear Structure (结构清晰) Ensure logical integrity and clear layers. Logical flow and easy to follow content.
Emotional Resonance (情感共鸣) Incorporate emotional elements. Viewer connection and deeper engagement.
Interactive Guidance (互动引导) Set up interactive topics or calls to action at the end. Increased user interaction and community engagement.
