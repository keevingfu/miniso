Brand Overview

MINISO, established in 2013, is a global retail brand originating from China. It offers a wide range of products including home goods, beauty, stationery, and electronics, positioned as "high-quality, low-price". Inspired by Japanese design philosophies, MINISO operates under the concept of "global selection, design innovation".

  • Founded: 2013
  • Founders: Ye Guofu, совместно с Miyake Junya (Japanese designer)
  • Headquarters: Guangzhou, China
  • Positioning: Lifestyle consumer brand with minimalist style
  • Product Features: High cost-performance, diverse categories, simple and fashionable design
  • Global Expansion: Thousands of stores worldwide, presence in Japan, USA, India, Brazil, and more.

Brand Culture & Marketing Strategies

MINISO's brand culture emphasizes globalization and localization, leveraging a light-asset supply chain for rapid expansion and market share capture. It combines fashion with practicality, collaborating with designers to create products relevant to daily consumer needs. Key strategies include:

  • Globalization & Localization: Adapting to global markets while maintaining brand consistency.
  • Fashion & Practicality: Products designed for daily use with trendy aesthetics.
  • Affordable Pricing: Low prices through mass production and streamlined supply chain.
  • Social Media & KOL Marketing: Engaging young consumers through social platforms and collaborations with Key Opinion Leaders.

KOL Marketing Strategies on Social Media

MINISO actively employs KOL marketing on YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok to promote product categories like Figurines & Dolls, Blind Boxes, and Bags. Strategies vary per platform and product type:

Figurines & Dolls KOL Marketing

Platforms: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok.

  • YouTube: Unboxing and review videos by lifestyle, shopping, and anime bloggers, focusing on cuteness and emotional appeal.
  • Instagram: Promotion by fashion, home decor, and parenting bloggers, showcasing integration into daily life and home settings.
  • TikTok: Short, engaging videos by micro-influencers, highlighting unique styles and launching "challenge activities" to attract young audiences.
  • Content Examples: Unboxing videos, collection showcases, integration with home decor, and cute styling.

Blind Box KOL Marketing

Platforms: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok.

  • YouTube: Collaboration with toy and collection bloggers for unboxing and blind box challenges, emphasizing surprise and variety.
  • Instagram: Visual content showcasing different blind box series, using discounts and shopping links, focusing on mystery and collectibility.
  • TikTok: Partnership with creative bloggers to highlight the randomness and surprise of blind boxes through creative short videos and "lucky draw" formats.
  • Content Examples: Blind box openings, collection reveals, lucky draws, and challenge videos.

Bags KOL Marketing

Platforms: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok.

  • YouTube: Fashion and lifestyle bloggers creating "shopping haul" videos, detailing design, usage scenarios, and value.
  • Instagram: Fashion and lifestyle influencers demonstrating bag styling, usage in daily outfits, and showcasing versatility.
  • TikTok: Short, creative videos by fashion bloggers demonstrating bag usage scenarios and style challenges to engage young users quickly.
  • Content Examples: Shopping hauls, outfit pairings, detailed bag reviews, and style challenges.
